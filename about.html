Since its establishment in 2007, Guru has grown into a publicly listed 4A's digital marketing agency — a true powerhouse driven by over 300 young, creative and future-ready minds across five global offices.

From strategy and creativity to media, data, technology and AI — Guru connects every dimension of modern marketing to unlock growth, accelerate relevance and create measurable business impact for ambitious brands worldwide.
